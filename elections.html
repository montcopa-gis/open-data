About this data

Voter Registration

Registration figures reflect Montgomery County's official voter rolls as of May 4, 2026. Data is sourced live from the county's GIS services and includes registered voters by party across all precincts. Independent and Other registrations are included in county totals.

Election Results

Results reflect official certified totals from Montgomery County general elections. Figures include Election Day, mail-in, absentee, and provisional ballots. Results are final and are not updated after certification. Additional election years will be added as they become available.

Data use & licensing

All election data published here is provided free for public use. Montgomery County makes no representations about suitability for any particular purpose. For official election administration matters, contact the Montgomery County Board of Elections.
