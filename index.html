Montgomery County, Pennsylvania

Open data.
Open government.

Montgomery County is committed to making government data transparent, accessible, and useful for everyone — residents, researchers, developers, and businesses alike.

Our open data portal provides free access to authoritative GIS datasets covering boundaries, parcels, transportation, elections, public safety, and more. All data is published and maintained by Montgomery County GIS and updated on a regular basis.

We believe that open data drives informed communities, supports civic innovation, and builds trust between government and the people it serves.
